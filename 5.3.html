Labour saving devices

Examples

Automatic Washing Machine
Microwave Oven
Cooker
Automatic Dishwasher
Robot Vacuum Cleaner
Bread-making Machine
Smart Fridge and Freezer

Pros and cons

Pros Cons
  • No longer have to do manual tasks at home
  • No need to stay home to wait until the food is done or the clothes are washed
  • More leisure time for hobbies and socialising
  • Give a sense of security (Automated burglar alarms)
  • Healthier lifestyles (Smart fridges and freezer)
  • Preventing food waste (Smart fridges and freezer)
  • Unhealthy lifestyles due to reliance on the devices
  • Make people lazy
  • Can become less fit
  • Potential to lose household skills

Other types of Microprocessor-controlled devices at home

Examples

Alarm Clock
TV sets
Central Heating and Air Conditioning System
Home Entertainment System

Pros and cons

Pros Cons
  • Save energy since they are efficient
  • Easy to program the devices to do tasks
  • Lead to a more wasteful society
  • Can be complex to operate for people who are not comfortable with electronic devices
  • Wasteful of electricity if the devices are left on stand-by
