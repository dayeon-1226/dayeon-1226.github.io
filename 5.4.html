Other safety rules needed to be carried out

  • Check the state of wires/cables and plugs
  • Make sure drinks are away from the computer
  • Fix wires along walls and behind desks
  • Do not cover computers with paper or fabric
  • Don't plug too many devices into an electric outlet socket - to reduce the risk of fire
  • Exercise every hour
  • Carry out an 'ergonomic assessment'
